AI Transformation Checklist (90 Days)

Phase 1: Discovery (Weeks 1–2)
- Map processes ripe for automation
- Audit data sources and access
- Define success metrics and guardrails

Phase 2: Pilot (Weeks 3–6)
- Prioritize 2–3 high-impact use-cases
- Build rapid prototypes and validate
- Set up logging and monitoring

Phase 3: Rollout (Weeks 7–10)
- Harden solution, add auth and roles
- Train staff and document SOPs
- Measure ROI against baseline

Phase 4: Optimize (Weeks 11–13)
- Iterate on UX and performance
- Expand to adjacent processes
- Plan continuous improvement cadence
